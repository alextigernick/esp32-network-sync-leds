▣ LED Spatial Calibration

Camera-based multi-panel spatial alignment. Detects each LED position in camera pixels, fits a similarity transform per device, computes relative offsets and rotations.

⚠ Camera blocked: not on HTTPS.
This page should be loaded over HTTPS so the browser allows camera access. The ESP32 serves HTTPS on port 443 with a self-signed certificate.

Go to: and accept the certificate warning once ("Advanced → Accept the Risk and Continue" in Firefox, or "Advanced → Proceed" in Chrome). Camera will work after that.

The cert is self-signed for 192.168.4.1 (the AP). Always use the AP IP for calibration.
